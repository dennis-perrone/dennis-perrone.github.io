# Risk Management Framework Roles and Responsibilities
Most of this was taken from the DoD Program Manager’s Guidebook for Integrating the Cybersecurity Risk Management Framework (RMF) into the System Acquisition Lifecycle which can be found [here](https://www.dau.edu/tools/Lists/DAUTools/Attachments/37/DoD%20-%20Guidebook,%20Cybersecurity%20Risk%20Management%20Framework,%20v1.08,%20Sep%202015.pdf).

* Authorizing Official (AO)
    * Responsible for authorizing the system’s operation based on achieving and maintaining an acceptable risk posture. 
    * Reference: [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
    * DIACAP term: Designated Accrediting Authority (DAA) 
* Developer
    * Role may be performed in-house, by another government entity, or by a contractor/system integrator. The developer should understand relevant threats and be able to assess mission needs and capability gaps against likely adversary threat capabilities. Development will be conducted in accordance with security controls related to assurance, *system development, and security best practices to reduce vulnerabilities and to design, build, and test security in the system early and cost effectively.
    * Reference: [DoDI 5000.02](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/500002p.pdf?ver=2020-01-23-144114-093)
* DoD Component Chief Information Officer (CIO)
    * Responsible for administration of the RMF within the DoD Component cybersecurity program; participation in the RMF Technical Advisory Group (TAG) visibility and sharing of the RMF status of assigned information system (IS) and PIT systems; and enforcement of training requirements for persons participating in the RMF.
    * Reference: [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
* Information Owner (IO)
    * Acts as statutory or operational authority for specified information; responsible for establishing the controls for data generation, classification, collection, processing, dissemination, and disposal.
    * Reference:
        * [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
        * [CNSSI 4009](https://rmf.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/CNSSI-4009.pdf)
* Information System Security Officer (ISSO)
    * Responsible for maintaining the appropriate operational security posture for an information system or program.
    * Reference:
        * [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
        * [CNSSI 4009](https://rmf.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/CNSSI-4009.pdf)
    * DIACAP term: Information Assurance Officer
* Information System Security Manager (ISSM)
    * Responsible for ensuring all products, services, and PIT have completed the appropriate evaluation and configuration processes prior to incorporation into or connection to an IS or PIT system.
    * Reference: [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
    * DIACAP term: Information Assurance Manager
* Program Executive Office
    * Responsible for executive management of assigned programs. Supervises design of acquisition programs, preparation of programs for decisions, and execution of approved program plans. 
    * Reference: [DoDI 5000.02](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/500002p.pdf?ver=2020-01-23-144114-093)
* Program Manager (System Manager)
    * Responsible for ensuring the program meets statutory and regulatory requirements for cybersecurity and for incorporating cybersecurity requirements into the program from  conceptual development through design and sustainment/disposal.
    * Reference: [DoDI 5000.02](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/500002p.pdf?ver=2020-01-23-144114-093)
* Security Control Assessor
    * Develops the Security Assessment Plan and ensures decomposed component security specifications, including verification criteria, are fully defined and traced to the controls delineated in the Security Plan.
    * Reference: [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
    * DIACAP term: Certifying Authority
* User Representative
    * Defines the system’s operational and functional requirements, and is responsible for ensuring that user operational interests are met throughout the system’s authorization process. 
    * Reference:
        * [DoDI 8510.01](https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/851001p.pdf?ver=2019-02-26-101520-300)
        * [CNSSI 4009](https://rmf.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/CNSSI-4009.pdf)
